Meet Tara

Tara Preston

Tara Preston is a Midlife Recalibration Guide, Sacred Nourishment Mentor, Woman's Wellness Speaker, Host of the Radiant Midlife Podcast, and Creator of the Flower of Life Akashic Method & Certification.

For over 15 years, Tara has guided women through profound personal transformation, helping them heal inherited patterns, reconnect with themselves, and step into new seasons of life with greater vitality, confidence, and self-trust.

Her work evolved through a deeply personal journey into nourishment, vitality, and midlife wellbeing — with foundations in holistic nutrition through the Canadian School of Natural Nutrition.

What Tara discovered is that many women aren't simply exhausted because they're busy. They're depleted because they've spent decades putting themselves last — conditioned to overgive, over-function, and carry responsibility for everyone around them.

Today, Tara's work focuses on helping women heal the Mother Wound in midlife through a unique blend of sacred nourishment, nervous system recalibration, embodiment practices, Akashic healing, and feminine leadership work.

She is the founder of the Akashic Women's School and creator of the Flower of Life Akashic Method — a feminine body-based healing pathway developed through more than 6,000 Akashic Record sessions.
